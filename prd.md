# 🛠️ Mechanical Shop Website PRD

---

# 🔹 PRODUCT OVERVIEW

This product is a **full-service mechanical shop website** designed to help customers easily:

* Discover services (oil change, tires, diagnostics, body work)
* Book appointments online
* Get estimates
* Contact the shop
* Build trust through reviews and transparency

The platform will evolve from a **simple booking website (V1)** into a **full digital garage ecosystem (V3)**.

---

# 🎯 GOALS

* Increase shop bookings
* Reduce phone call overload
* Improve customer experience
* Digitize operations
* Build a strong local online presence (SEO + Google ranking)

### 1. Customers

* Car owners needing services

### 2. Shop Owner
